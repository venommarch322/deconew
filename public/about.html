About Aura & Abode

We believe extraordinary rooms do not require extraordinary budgets. Aura & Abode was founded on the conviction that a single well-executed decor tip can completely transform how a room looks and feels — and that this knowledge should be free, accessible, and explained with enough detail that anyone can implement it confidently.

Our articles include specific measurements, step-by-step instructions, and the underlying design principles that explain why techniques work. You are not just following recipes — you are building genuine decorating intuition.

We are completely independent. No brand sponsorships influence our recommendations. Every piece of advice is based purely on what works, what looks beautiful, and what creates the biggest transformation for the least effort.
